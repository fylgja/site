A media card pairs an image with text in one surface, ideal for a featured post or
banner. It builds on the [Card](/ui/layout/card/): the image goes in a `<picture>`
and the text in a `.card-content` block, and `md:flex` lays them side by side once
there is room.

## Usage

- Put the media (a `<picture>` or `<img>`) as a direct child of the card, and the
  text in a `<div class="card-content">`. With a `.card-content` present, Fylgja
  moves the padding there so the media bleeds to the card edges.
- Add `md:flex` to the card and `flex-none` to the media so they sit side by side
  on wider screens and stack on small ones.
- `object-cover` keeps the image filling its box; `line-clamp` trims long text.

## Accessibility

- Give the image a descriptive `alt`.
- Use a real heading inside `.card-content` so the card appears in the outline.
